A Day in the Life of a Dental Assistant

What Dental Assistants Do

Engaging with Patients

You'll have friendly chats with patients, addressing their concerns and ensuring they feel comfortable.

Handling Office Admin Tasks

Alongside patient care, you'll take care of essential administrative duties that keep the practice running smoothly.

Assisting Dentists & Hygenists

Working closely with dentists, you'll support them during procedures like fillings, extractions, and more.

Taking Dental Impressions

You'll become skilled at taking dental impressions, an important part of the patient's treatment process.
